Udaranga Wickramasinghe

I am currently a fourth-year PhD student at CVLAB, EPFL. I work under the supervision of Prof. Pascal Fua. Prior to joining CVLAB, I completed my master's degree in Computer Science at EPFL, Switzerland in 2017 and bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka in 2014

Research

I am interested in Computer Vision and Machine Learning with a focus in medical image analysis. I mainly work on extracting accurate and better quality (usable) meshes from image stacks (3D) and images (2D). I am also interested in ways to introduce prior knowledge in to deep neural networks. With these approaches, I indirectly explore the problem of training large networks with small amount of labeled data.
